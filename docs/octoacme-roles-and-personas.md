# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads ensure product quality through comprehensive testing strategies, test automation, and acceptance validation. They work with developers and product leads to define quality standards and catch issues before release.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Build and maintain automated test suites (unit, integration, end-to-end)
- Triage and document defects with reproducibility steps
- Validate release readiness before production deployment
- Advise on testability during design and code review phases

### Goals
- Minimize production defects and customer-facing issues
- Provide fast feedback to accelerate deployment cycles
- Enable confidence in release quality

### Typical Communication
- Daily standup participation and defect reports
- Test plan reviews with developers and product leads
- Pre-release smoke test execution and sign-off

---

## Scrum Master/Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach teams on iterative delivery practices. They focus on process improvement and psychological safety.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and escalate team blockers and impediments
- Coach the team on agile principles and continuous improvement
- Track velocity and help identify capacity constraints
- Protect the team from mid-sprint scope creep

### Goals
- Maintain team velocity and predictability
- Foster psychological safety and feedback culture
- Reduce process friction and decision-making delays

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Impediment tracking and escalation
- Retro action item follow-up

---

## Design/UX Lead

### Role Summary
Design/UX Leads define user experience, visual design, and usability standards. They collaborate with product and engineering to ensure solutions are both functional and user-centric.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and define user personas
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Define accessibility and design system standards
- Conduct usability testing and iterate based on feedback
- Review implementation against design specifications

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Maintain consistent design language and quality

### Typical Communication
- Design critiques and feedback sessions
- Weekly sync with product and engineering leads
- Usability testing reports and recommendations

---

## Security Officer

### Role Summary
Security Officers ensure that products and processes meet security standards and compliance requirements. They advise on threat mitigation and security architecture.

### Responsibilities
- Review security requirements during project initiation
- Conduct threat modeling and architecture reviews
- Configure and monitor security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
- Triage and advise on security vulnerabilities
- Define secure coding standards and practices
- Manage incident response and post-incident reviews

### Goals
- Prevent security incidents and data breaches
- Meet compliance and regulatory requirements
- Build security awareness across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Security review checkpoints in sprint planning and pre-release
- Vulnerability reports and remediation timelines
- Incident response coordination

---

## Operations/DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Operations/DevOps Engineers manage infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and system reliability. They enable fast, safe deployments and monitor production systems.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure
- Manage deployments to staging and production environments
- Monitor system health, performance, and error rates
- Manage incident response and post-mortem follow-up
- Advise on scalability and reliability requirements during planning

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable deployments with zero-downtime capability
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Provide rapid incident response and recovery

### Typical Communication
- Pre-release deployment planning
- Post-deploy verification and monitoring
- Incident alerts and status updates

---

## Stakeholder/Executive Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Executive Sponsors represent business interests, provide resources, and make strategic decisions. They remove organizational barriers and ensure alignment with business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project charter and business case
- Allocate budget and resources
- Escalate organizational blockers and dependencies
- Provide executive visibility and stakeholder alignment
- Make trade-off decisions when scope, timeline, or resources conflict

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers business value
- Maximize resource utilization and minimize waste
- Enable cross-organization coordination

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and review sessions
- Decision-making on escalated issues
- Budget and resource approval meetings
